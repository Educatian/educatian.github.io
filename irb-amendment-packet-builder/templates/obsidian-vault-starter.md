---

## Project note sections

1. Study purpose
2. Participant population
3. Current approved procedures
4. Proposed change
5. Data elements
6. AI/API boundaries
7. Instruments and appendices
8. GitHub/repo evidence
9. Open questions for PI/IRB

## Safe vault rule

Keep the vault as an index and drafting workspace. Do not store raw participant data, identifiable exports, private consent forms, or sensitive IRB correspondence in a public or synced vault unless your institution has approved that storage path.
